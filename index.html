Orange County Tech Events

Just a collection of tech events I know of in Orange County.

  • OC Tech Link - Meets every other Wednesday at 6pm at Hangar 24 in Irvine. Been to this, great crowd. Social meetup.
  • tech night! - Newer event based in Irvine. Been to this, GREAT crowd.
  • Irvine Underground - More technical group from Irvine, meets 2nd Friday of the month at 7pm. More established group.
  • OC 2600 - Fullerton based, meet 1st Friday of the month at 7.
  • 23b Shop - Fullerton based, meet 1st Friday of the month at 7 with OC 2600.
  • Friends of Figma - UI/UX group. Varying schedule. Irvine based.
  • Socal UX Designers - UI/UX group. Varying schedule. Irvine Based.
